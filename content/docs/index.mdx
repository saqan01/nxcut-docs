## Finding your way around

When you log in, you'll see a menu on the left side of the screen. Each item takes you to a different part of NxCut.

| Section | What it's for | Who can see it |
|---------|--------------|----------------|
| [Dashboard](/docs/dashboard) | Business overview and key numbers | Admins only |
| [Calendar](/docs/calendar) | Appointments and bookings | All staff |
| [Sales](/docs/sales) | Sales history and transactions | All staff |
| [Clients](/docs/clients) | Client directory and profiles | All staff |
| [Catalog](/docs/catalog) | Services, products, and packages | All staff |
| [Team](/docs/team) | Staff profiles and schedules | All staff |
| [Commission](/docs/commission) | Earnings and salary slips | Admins only |
| [Reports](/docs/reporting) | Financial reports and analytics | Admins only |
| [Settings](/docs/settings) | Business configuration | Admins only |

<Callout type="info">
**Admin vs. Staff** — Admin users can access everything. Regular staff only see what they need for their daily work: Calendar, Sales, Clients, Catalog, and Team.
</Callout>
