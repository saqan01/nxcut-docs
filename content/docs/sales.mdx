title: Sales Hub
description: View, filter, and export your full sales history — appointments, payments, memberships, vouchers, products, and tips

The Sales Hub is your complete record of everything that's been sold or paid for. Click **Sales** in the left menu and you'll see a sidebar with eight sections. Each one is focused on a different part of your business and has its own search, date filter, and **Export** button.

---

## Daily Sales

**Sales → Daily Sales**

Your end-of-day summary. Use the **Today**, **&lt;**, and **&gt;** buttons to pick any day and see a full breakdown of what happened.

The page is split into three parts:

**Transaction summary**
A table showing what was sold by item type (e.g., Service, Product), how many were sold, and the net total.

**Cash movement summary**
A breakdown of how payments were collected — showing each payment method (Cash, Online, Card, etc.) and the amount collected through each. Also shows the total **Payments collected** and how much of that was **tips**.

**Summary bar**
Four quick-view numbers at the bottom:
- **Total Sales** — total revenue for the day
- **Total Transactions** — number of completed transactions
- **Sale Types** — how many different item types were sold
- **Payment Methods** — how many different payment methods were used

## Appointments

**Sales → Appointments**

A full list of every appointment-based sale. Use this when you need to look up a specific service, client visit, or check what was charged and whether a discount was applied.

**Columns:** Sale ID · Client (name + phone) · Service · Sale Date · Team Member · Unit Price · Quantity · Discount · Total Price

Discounts show in red when applied. Search by client name or service, or filter by date range. Click **Export** to download as CSV.

---

## Sales Transactions

**Sales → Sales**

Your complete transaction history across all sale types. By default it shows the last 30 days.

**Columns:** Sale ID · Payment Date · Client · Team Members · Payment Method · Tips · Status · Amount

The **Status** column shows either:
- **Completed** — the sale went through successfully
- **Voided** (red badge) — the sale was reversed after the fact

Click any **Sale ID** to open the full [Sale Details panel](#sale-details).

Click any **Client name** to open their [Client Profile](#client-profile).

---

## Payments

**Sales → Payments**

Every payment transaction that was successfully collected — similar to Sales Transactions but focused on the payment side only. Voided sales are excluded here.

**Columns:** Sale ID · Payment Date · Client · Team Members · Payment Method · Tips · Status · Amount

Tips show in the Tips column when they were added to the sale. Search by client name or sale ID, or filter by date range.

---

## Memberships

**Sales → Memberships**

All membership packages that have been sold, with live session tracking.

**Columns:** Purchase Date · Client · Membership name · Service · Sessions (used / remaining with a progress bar) · Cost · Status

The **Status** shows either **Active** (sessions still available) or **Completed** (all sessions used). The progress bar makes it easy to see at a glance how far through a package a client is.

---

## Vouchers

**Sales → Vouchers**

All vouchers that have been purchased or redeemed. Search by client name, voucher name, or voucher code. Filter by date range to narrow results.

---

## Products

**Sales → Products**

A record of every product sold, with live stock levels.

**Columns:** Sale ID · Product Name · Category · Brand · Quantity · Unit Price · Total Price · Current Stock · Sale Date · Status

The **Current Stock** column shows a warning icon (⚠) when stock is running low. The **Status** badge will show **Low Stock** to flag items that need reordering.

Search by product name, SKU, barcode, or category.

---

## Tips

**Sales → Tips**

Every tip that's been collected, attributed to the staff member who received it.

**Columns:** Sale ID · Tip Amount · Staff Member · Payment Method · Sale Total · Date

Search by staff name, payment method, or sale ID. Filter by date range.

---

## Client Profile

Anywhere you see a **client name** shown as a blue link (in Sales Transactions, Payments, etc.), you can click it to open that client's full profile in a panel on the right.

The profile is split into two parts:

**Left — Client details**
- Name, phone, and email
- **Edit client** — update their contact details
- **Add review** — add an internal note or review about the client
- Date of birth (optional)
- Date the client was created in the system

**Middle — Navigation tabs**
Switch between different views of the client's history:

| Tab | What it shows |
|-----|--------------|
| Appointments | All appointments with status badges (Paid, Scheduled, No Show) |
| Sales | All completed sales with service, total, and a View Details button |
| Products | Products purchased by this client |
| Vouchers | Vouchers associated with this client |
| Memberships | Active and completed membership packages (shows count badge) |
| Loyalty | Loyalty programme activity |

**Right — Detail view**
Clicking any item in the tabs shows the full detail on the right side. For example, clicking a sale shows the service, time, staff, subtotal, VAT, and total.

## Sale Details

Clicking a **Sale ID** or **View Details** anywhere in the Sales Hub opens the full Sale Details panel. This shows:

- **Status badge** (e.g., Completed)
- Client name and phone
- **VAT Invoice** number and date
- Services performed with time, duration, and assigned team
- **Staff Information** — assigned staff member and receptionist
- Subtotal, VAT (5%), and Total
- **Print icons** — print as thermal receipt or standard A4 invoice
- **Sale-ID** (copyable)
- **Void Sale** button — reverses the transaction if an error was made
- **Back to Appointment** button — returns to the linked appointment

<Callout type="warning">
Voiding a sale cannot be undone. Only use this if there was a genuine error with the transaction.
</Callout>
