The Team Management page is where you manage everyone who works at your salon — their profiles, working hours, service permissions, and login accounts.

Click **Team** in the left menu to get started. The page is divided into two sections: **Team Members** and **Authenticated Users**.

---

## Team Members

![Team Members](/19_team_staff.gif)

Team Members are the staff profiles that appear on the Calendar. Each card shows the staff member's photo (or initials if no photo is set), their email, and phone number.

Each card has three action buttons:
- **Edit Shift** — set their weekly working hours
- **Edit** — update their profile details
- **Delete** (red trash icon) — remove them permanently

To view archived staff, toggle **Show archived team members only** in the top right corner.

---

## Adding a team member

Click **+ Add → Add Team Member**. A form will pop up.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click the **+** circle at the top to upload a profile photo (JPEG or PNG, under 2 MB). This is optional but recommended — it helps identify staff on the Calendar.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Enter their **First Name** (required) and **Last Name**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Set a **Calendar Color** — this is the color used for their column on the Calendar. Each staff member should have a distinct color to make them easy to tell apart.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Optionally enter a **Team Member ID** — useful if your business uses internal staff ID numbers.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Set **Category Permissions** — toggle on the service categories this staff member is allowed to perform (e.g., Hair, Nails, Facial). All categories are enabled by default. Staff will only appear as a booking option for the categories you enable.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Toggle **Visible to Clients** on if this staff member should appear in client-facing booking. This is on by default.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Toggle **Admin User** on if they need full access to the system (Reports, Settings, Commission, Dashboard). Off by default.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Select a **Location** (required).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Add Team Member**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing a team member

Click **Edit** on any team member card to update their photo, name, calendar color, category permissions, admin status, or location.

<Callout type="warning">
You cannot remove admin access from the last remaining admin. Make sure at least one other admin exists before making that change.
</Callout>

---

## Setting working hours (Edit Shift)

![Team Schedule](/20_team_schedule.gif)

Each staff member can have their own weekly schedule so the Calendar only shows them as available when they're actually working.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click **Edit Shift** on the team member's card.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    For each day of the week, set their **start** and **finish time**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Mark any day as **Off** if they don't work that day.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Save**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Authenticated Users

![Team Admin Users](/21_team_admin.gif)

The **Authenticated Users** section sits below the team member grid and shows all staff login accounts. These cards have an **orange border** to distinguish them from team member profiles.

Each card shows the user's name, their **Auth User** label, email address, and Location ID.

<Callout type="info">
A team member profile and a login account are two separate things. A team member profile controls how someone appears on the Calendar. A login account controls how they log in to NxCut. Most staff will need both.
</Callout>

---

## Adding a login account (Auth User)

Click **+ Add → Add Auth User**. A form will pop up.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Enter their **Email** (required) — this will be their username when logging in.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Set a **Password** (required).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Optionally enter a **Display Name** and **Phone Number**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Toggle **Admin User** on if they should have full system access.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Select a **Location** (required).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Create User**. The account is active immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Archiving a team member

If someone leaves but you want to keep their history in the system, archive them instead of deleting.

- Open their profile and choose **Archive**.
- Archived members are hidden from the default view.
- Toggle **Show archived team members only** at the top of the page to find them again.

---

## Quick reference

| What do you want to do? | How? |
|-------------------------|------|
| Add a new staff member | Click **+ Add → Add Team Member** |
| Give them a login to NxCut | Click **+ Add → Add Auth User** |
| Set when they work each week | Click **Edit Shift** on their card |
| Control which services they can do | Edit their profile and toggle the service categories |
| Stop them appearing to clients | Edit their profile and turn off **Visible to Clients** |
| Give someone full access to everything | Turn on **Admin User** in their profile |
| Hide a staff member who left | Use **Archive** — their history stays, they just disappear from the main view |
