The Client List is your salon's client database. Every person who visits your salon can have a profile here, making it easy to look them up, track their history, and manage their memberships and vouchers.

Click **Clients** in the left menu to get started.

---

## The client list

The main view shows a table of all your clients with the following columns:

| Column | What it shows |
|--------|--------------|
| Client name | Name with an avatar (initials if no photo) |
| Mobile number | Their phone number |
| Reviews | Internal reviews added by your team |
| Sales | Total amount spent across all visits |
| Created at | When the client was added to the system |

Use the **search bar** at the top to find anyone by name, email, or phone number.

The list is paginated — use the **Previous / Next** buttons at the bottom to navigate, and adjust the **Show** dropdown to control how many results appear per page.

---

## Adding a new client

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click the **+ Add** button in the top right corner. A form will pop up.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Fill in the client's details:
    - **First Name** (required)
    - **Last Name**
    - **Phone Number** (required — select the country code from the dropdown)
    - **Email**
    - **Date of Birth**
    - **Notes** — any internal notes about the client
    - **Location** — assign them to a specific branch
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Save Client**. They'll appear in the list and be available for booking straight away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip">
You can also add a client on the spot while booking an appointment in the Calendar — no need to come here first.
</Callout>

## Viewing a client's profile

Click on any client's name to open their full profile in a panel. The profile has three sections:

**Left — Client details**
- Name, phone, and email
- **Edit client** — update any of their details
- **Add review** — leave an internal note or review about this client
- Date of birth (can be added here if not set)
- Date the client was added to the system

**Middle — Navigation tabs**
Switch between different parts of the client's history:

| Tab | What it shows |
|-----|--------------|
| Appointments | All appointments with status (Paid, Scheduled, No Show, etc.) |
| Sales | All completed sales with service name, total, and View Details |
| Products | Products purchased by this client |
| Vouchers | Vouchers linked to this client |
| Memberships | Active and completed membership packages with session counts |
| Loyalty | Loyalty programme activity |

**Right — Detail view**
Clicking any item in the tabs shows the full detail. For example, clicking a sale opens the VAT invoice with the service, staff, subtotal, VAT, and total — with options to print or void the sale.
