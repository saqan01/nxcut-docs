The Reports section gives you a deeper look at your salon's performance beyond the day-to-day. All reports are filterable, exportable, and organised by category so you can find what you need quickly.

Click **Reports** in the left menu to get started.

<Callout type="info">
Reports are only accessible to admin users.
</Callout>

---

## Finding a report

The main reports page shows all available reports as cards. You can:

- **Search** by report name or description
- **Filter by creator** using the All creators dropdown
- **Filter by type** using the All reports dropdown
- **Browse by category** using the tabs across the top: All reports · Sales · Finance · Appointments · Team · Clients · Inventory

The left sidebar organises reports into:

| Category | What's in it |
|----------|-------------|
| All reports | Every report in one place |
| Favourites | Reports you've starred for quick access |
| Dashboards | Visual overview reports (e.g. Performance dashboard) |
| Premium | Premium-tier reports |
| Folders | Custom folders you create to organise reports |

Star any report card to add it to your **Favourites**.

## Available reports

### Performance Dashboard
*(Dashboards)*

A visual overview of your salon's performance. Shows key metrics, charts, and trends across revenue, appointments, services, and staff — all in one place.

---

### Sales Log
*(Sales)*

A detailed record of every sale — including the services sold, discounts applied, payment methods used, staff member, and client. Use this when you need to trace a specific transaction or review sales over a period.

**Filters:** Date range, location, team member, client, payment method.

**Export:** CSV, Excel, PDF.

---

### Finance Summary
*(Finance)*

A period-by-period breakdown of your sales, payments, and liabilities. Perfect for month-end reviews and accounting.

Choose how to group the data:

| Period | What you'll see |
|--------|----------------|
| Day | Last 30 days, one column per day |
| Week | Last 4 weeks |
| Month | Last 6 months (default) |
| Quarter | Last 4 quarters |
| Year | Last 3 years |

**Export:** CSV, Excel, PDF.

---

### Payment Transactions
*(Finance)*

A full log of every individual payment collected. Use this to verify payment method totals or trace a specific transaction.

**Filters:** Date range, location, team member, payment method, transaction type, amount range.

**Export:** CSV, Excel, PDF.

---

### Tips Summary
*(Team)*

An overview of all tips received, broken down by staff member. Shows total tips, number of tipped transactions, average tip, and tip percentage for each person.

**Filters:** Date range, location, team member, payment method.

**Export:** CSV, Excel, PDF.

---

## Export options

All reports can be exported in:

| Format | Best for |
|--------|---------|
| CSV | Importing into a spreadsheet |
| Excel (.xlsx) | A formatted, ready-to-share spreadsheet |
| PDF | Printing or sharing outside the system |

---

## Date range options

All reports use the same date picker with quick presets:

- Today / Yesterday
- Last 7, 30, or 90 days
- Last week / Last month / Last 3, 6, or 12 months
- Week-to-date / Month-to-date / Quarter-to-date / Year-to-date
- Custom start and end date
