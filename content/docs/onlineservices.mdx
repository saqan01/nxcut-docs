---
title: Online Services
description: How to create online services and control which services are visible on your booking page
---

Online Services lets you control exactly which services appear on your salon's public booking page. You can create dedicated online-only services with their own pricing, and toggle visibility for any service at any time.

Click **Online Services** in the left menu to get started.

---

## Creating an online service

![Create Online Service](/online_services/create_services.gif)

Online services are separate listings from your internal catalog — they're what clients see and select when booking through your website.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Online Services** and click **Add Service**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Give the service a **name** and choose a **category** to group it correctly on the booking page.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Set the **duration** and **price** that clients will see.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Save** to publish it to your booking page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Checking which services are visible online

![Service Online Visibility](/online_services/service_online_check.gif)

You can review and control which services are currently showing on your booking page at any time.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Online Services** to see the full list of your services and their online visibility status.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Use the toggle next to each service to turn it **on** or **off** on the booking page.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Changes take effect immediately — no need to save separately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Only services with their toggle set to active will appear when clients visit your booking page. If a service isn't showing up online, check that it's toggled on here.
</Callout>

## Setting which team members are visible online

![Service Team Online Visibility](/online_services/online-team-member.gif)

You can control which team members appear on your booking page, and which services each team member is bookable for.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to the **Team Members** page and open the team member you want to configure.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Under **Category Permissions**, you'll see a toggle for each service category (e.g. Hair, Massage, Nail). Turn a category **on** to allow clients to book that team member for services in that category online. Turn it **off** to hide them from that category on the booking page.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Make sure **Visible to Clients** is toggled **on** — this controls whether the team member appears on the booking page at all. If this is off, they won't show up regardless of the category settings.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Save Changes** to apply.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
A team member will only appear for a service online if both their **Visible to Clients** toggle is on and the relevant **Category Permission** is enabled. If a team member isn't showing up for a specific service, check both settings.
</Callout>
