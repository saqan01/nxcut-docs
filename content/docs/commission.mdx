The Commission page is where you define how much each staff member earns from the services they perform, and view a live breakdown of what they've earned. NxCut calculates everything automatically based on real sales data.

Click **Commission** in the left menu to get started.

<Callout type="info">
Commission is only accessible to admin users.
</Callout>

The page is split into two sections: **Team Member Commissions** (your commission rules) and **Commission Calculations** (the live earnings breakdown).

---

## Team Member Commissions

This section lists all the commission rules you've set up. If no rules have been created yet, it will show "No commission settings found — add a commission setting to get started."

---

## Setting up a commission rule

Click **+ Add Commission** in the top right. A form will pop up.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Select a **Team Member** from the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    The **Services Commission** section is enabled by default. This means the staff member earns commission on the services they perform.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Choose the **Default commission type**:
    - **Fixed rate** — a set percentage applied to every sale they make
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Enter the **Default rate** — the percentage they'll earn (e.g., 30%).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Set the **Sales period** — how often commission is calculated. The default is **Monthly**. NxCut will show you exactly when the first and following periods will be.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Use the **Sample calculation** box to double-check your setup. Enter a sale amount and NxCut will show you the exact commission calculation — including the threshold, commissionable amount, and final payout.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Create Commission** to save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip">
The sample calculation updates in real time as you type — use it to make sure the numbers look right before saving.
</Callout>

## Commission Calculations

Below the commission rules is the **Commission Calculations** table — a live breakdown of what each staff member has earned for the current period.

At the top you'll see four summary cards:

| Card | What it shows |
|------|--------------|
| Team Members | Number of staff with sales in this period |
| Total Sales | Combined revenue across all staff |
| Total Commission | Total commission owed to the team |
| Avg Commission % | Average commission rate across the team |

The table below shows each staff member with:
- **Sales Count** — number of sales completed
- **Total Sales** — their total revenue
- **Commission Type** — their commission setup, or a **No Setup** badge if none has been configured
- **Commission Rate** — their percentage
- **Commission Amount** — what they've earned
- **Actions** — an eye icon to view the breakdown, and a salary slip icon to open their slip

Click **Export CSV** to download the full table for payroll processing.

---

## Salary Slip

Click the salary slip icon next to any team member to open their **Salary Slip**. This is a full payroll breakdown that can be filled in and downloaded.

The slip is headed with your salon's name and labelled as **Payroll Advice**. It shows the staff member's name, their occupancy percentage, free hours, the month, and their title.

**Earnings section**

A table where you can enter values for each earning type:

| Earning | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Basic Salary | Their fixed monthly salary |
| Over Time | Overtime hours worked |
| Voucher | Commission from voucher sales |
| Allowance | Any allowances paid |
| Products | Commission from product sales |
| Bonus | Any bonus payment |
| EOS | End of service accrual |
| Target | Target-based incentives |

Each row has editable fields for rate, days, and hours. The **Total Earning** is calculated automatically.

**Deductions section**

Enter any amounts to deduct:
- Deduction
- Cash advance
- Additional deduction

The **Total Deduction** and final **Net Salary** are calculated automatically at the bottom.

Once the slip is complete, click **Download XLS** to save it as a spreadsheet, or **Close** to exit without saving.
